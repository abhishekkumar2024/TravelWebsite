Top Hotels and Hostels in Jaipur 2026

Luxury, Heritage & Budget Accommodations

Jaipur, renowned as the Pink City of Rajasthan, ranks among India’s most frequented travel spots. With its majestic palaces, lively markets, and historic forts, the city draws luxury travellers, families, couples, backpackers, and solo adventurers alike. Jaipur is not only famous for its big forts and royal architecture, but it also offers the world's best five-star hotels and heritage places to stay.

If you are organising a trip and looking for the finest hotels in Jaipur, this comprehensive 2026 guide will assist you in selecting the ideal accommodation — whether you seek royal heritage, five-star hotels in Jaipur, boutique luxury, mid-range comfort, or budget-friendly hostels.

This guide includes the leading hotels in Jaipur, recommendations for where to stay in Jaipur, and advice for a seamless hotel booking experience.

Luxury Hotels in Jaipur, Rajasthan

If you want to experience royal hospitality, Jaipur offers some of India’s finest 5-star hotels in Jaipur.

1. Rambagh Palace

Rambagh Palace is one of the most iconic luxury heritage hotels in Jaipur, often called the “Jewel of Jaipur.” Once the royal residence of the Maharaja of Jaipur, this grand palace is now managed by the Taj Group and offers one of the most luxurious stays in India.

2. Jai Mahal Palace

Jai Mahal Palace is a stunning 5-star heritage hotel in Jaipur, managed by the Taj Group. Built in 1745, this Indo-Saracenic palace sits amid 18 acres of Mughal gardens, offering a royal yet serene stay experience in the heart of the Pink City.

3.The Oberoi Rajvilas

The Oberoi Rajvilas is one of the most luxurious and exclusive 5-star resorts in Jaipur. Spread across 32 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens, this palace-style resort offers a private, serene, and ultra-luxury experience inspired by traditional Rajasthani architecture.

It is consistently ranked among the best luxury hotels in India and the world.

4. Rajmahal Palace RASS

Rajmahal Palace RAAS is a luxury heritage hotel located on Sardar Patel Marg in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Originally built in 1729 as a royal residence for Maharaja Sawai Man ‘Jai’ Singh and later home to Maharani Gayatri Devi, the palace is now part of the RAAS collection of boutique hotels. It combines regal Art Deco design with contemporary comfort, offering an immersive glimpse into Jaipur’s aristocratic past.

5. Samode Haveli

Samode Haveli is a heritage hotel in the old walled city of Jaipur, India. Originally built over two centuries ago as the urban manor of the Samode royal family, it has been converted into a luxury boutique property that preserves its Indo-Saracenic architecture and aristocratic character. The haveli offers travellers a glimpse of Rajasthan’s regal past through its frescoed halls, courtyards, and personalised hospitality.

Five-Star Hotels in Jaipur (Modern Luxury)

If you prefer modern comfort over heritage charm, these are among the top hotels in Jaipur:

1. Hilton Jaipur

Hilton Jaipur is a modern 5-star business and leisure hotel located in the heart of Jaipur city. Unlike palace-style heritage hotels, Hilton offers contemporary luxury, international comfort standards, and strong brand reliability.

2. Jaipur Marriott Hotel

Jaipur Marriott Hotel is one of the most premium 5-star business and luxury hotels in Jaipur, located near Jaipur International Airport. Known for its modern elegance, large event spaces, and excellent dining options, it is a preferred choice for corporate travellers, weddings, and luxury stays.

3. ITC Rajputana

ITC Rajputana is a premium 5-star luxury hotel in Jaipur that blends modern comfort with traditional Rajasthani architecture. Inspired by the royal havelis of Rajasthan, this hotel is one of the most popular luxury stays in the Pink City.

Best Mid-Range Hotels in Jaipur City

1. Royal Orchid Central Jaipur

Royal Orchid Central Jaipur is one of the best hotels in Jaipur city for business and leisure travellers. It offers comfortable rooms, a central location, and good dining options. Best hotel for budget luxury seekers.

2. Regenta Central Jaipur

Regenta Central Jaipur is a well-rated 4-star business and leisure hotel in Jaipur's Jhotwara area. It offers comfortable mid-range luxury with modern amenities, making it a popular choice for business travellers, wedding guests, and budget-conscious tourists.

3. Zone by The Park Jaipur

Zone by The Park Jaipur is a modern 4-star lifestyle hotel located near Bani Park and MI Road, offering stylish interiors, affordable luxury, and a vibrant city vibe. It is popular among young travellers, business guests, and mid-range tourists looking for comfort at a reasonable price.

Best Budget Hostels in Jaipur

1. Zostel Jaipur

Zostel Jaipur is one of the most popular backpacker hostels in Jaipur, located in the heart of the Pink City. It is known for its vibrant social atmosphere, budget-friendly pricing, and heritage-style interiors, making it a favourite among solo travellers, backpackers, and young tourists.

2. Moustache Jaipur

Moustache Jaipur is a popular backpacker hostel located near MI Road and Bani Park in Jaipur. Known for its vibrant atmosphere, colourful interiors, and affordable pricing, it is a favourite among solo travellers, digital nomads, and young tourists visiting the Pink City.

3. The Hosteller Jaipur

The Hosteller Jaipur is a stylish and affordable backpacker hostel located in the heart of the Pink City. Known for its vibrant interiors, social atmosphere, and budget-friendly pricing, it is a popular choice among solo travellers, digital nomads, and young explorers visiting Jaipur.

Jaipur Hotel Room Booking Tips (2026)

To get the best deals:

1. Book 2–3 weeks in advance during peak season
2. Compare prices on multiple platforms
3. Check cancellation policy
4. Look for breakfast-included deals
5. Visit during weekdays for better rates

Peak Season:
October to March

Off-Season:
April to September (best for discounted prices)

FAQ

1. Which are the best hotels in Jaipur?

A. Some of the best hotels in Jaipur include Rambagh Palace, Jai Mahal Palace, Royal Orchid Central Jaipur, and Oberoi Rajvilas.

2. What are the best 5-star hotels in Jaipur?

A. Top 5-star hotels in Jaipur include Jaipur Marriott, ITC Rajputana, Hilton Jaipur, and luxury heritage hotels like Rambagh Palace.

3. Are there budget hotels in Jaipur city?

A. Yes, Jaipur offers many budget options, including Zostel Jaipur, Moustache Jaipur, and affordable hotels in Bani Park.

4. What is the best area to stay in Jaipur?

A. MI Road and C-Scheme are ideal for central access, while Amer Road is best for luxury and heritage stays.
